## Reclaim Your Attention

### The Problem

Average person spends 6+ hours on screens daily. Much of it is passive consumption.

### The Solution: Digital Minimalism

**Core Principles:**

1. **Intentional Use** - Every app has a purpose
2. **Quality > Quantity** - Deep work over endless scrolling
3. **Boundaries** - Set clear limits
4. **Tools, Not Masters** - Technology serves you

### Practical Steps

#### Phone
- Remove social apps
- Use grayscale mode
- Set app time limits
- Keep phone out of bedroom

#### Computer
- Use focus modes
- Block distracting websites
- Consolidate tools
- Automate repetitive tasks

#### Notifications
- Turn off non-essential
- Batch check times
- Use Do Not Disturb
- Keep only urgent alerts

### Tools That Help

- **Freedom** - Block websites
- **Cold Turkey** - Strict blocking
- **RescueTime** - Track usage
- **Forest** - Stay focused

### Benefits

- More free time
- Better focus
- Improved mental health
- Higher productivity
- Deeper relationships
