Be Specific

Instead of "write code," say "write a Python function that..."

Give Context

Tell the AI who you are and what you're trying to accomplish.

Use Examples

Few-shot prompting with examples dramatically improves results.

Iterate

First output rarely perfect. Refine based on results.
