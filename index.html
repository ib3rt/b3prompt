‚ö° Quick Tips

üéØ Be Specific

"Write about dogs" ‚Üí "Write a 500-word article about how Golden Retrievers make great family pets, focusing on their temperament with children."

üìã Use Structure

Break complex requests into numbered steps or bullet points. AI follows instructions better when they're organized.

üé≠ Define Persona

"Act as a senior software engineer with 20 years experience" gets better technical answers than a generic request.

üîÑ Iterate

First prompt rarely perfect. Refine based on output. Treat it as a conversation, not a one-shot.

üìä Provide Context

The AI doesn't know your situation. Include relevant background, constraints, and goals.

‚úÖ Verify Output

AI can hallucinate. Always verify facts, especially for important decisions or technical information.
